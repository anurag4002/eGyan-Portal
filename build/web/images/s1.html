Institutional Effectiveness

Monitoring, assessing and reviewing the University’s progress is key to ensuring the overall growth and development of faculty and students. At Amity University Dubai, we regularly evaluate performance and seek out areas for improvement of teaching, learning, organizational processes and more. 

As an education provider, we focus on being intentional about innovation and change, striving to create methodologies and systems that are effective, inspiring and always improving.  

Quality Assurance


Academic standards are maintained through the creation of policies and procedures to give students and faculty an overall high-quality teaching and learning experience. We continue to achieve expected outcomes by keeping up to date with local and international quality assurance standards in education. 

Centre for Teaching and Learning

The Centre for Teaching and Learning supports Amity University Dubai’s teaching staff as they work on creating student-centric and contemporary teaching methods. The Centre provides one-to-one sessions, encourages the exchange of internal and external best practices and supplies learning resources to faculty. Reflective sessions to monitor progress of pedagogy through feedback are conducted periodically. Strategic programme enhancement initiatives are also in place to ensure teaching and learning excellence. 


  • Support faculty to develop student-centered learning experiences
  • Fosters scholarships for teaching and learning
  • Promotes the deployment of education technology
  • Facilitates the sharing of best practices  
  • Inducts new faculty to the University’s teaching and learning practices
